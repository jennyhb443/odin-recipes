Strawberry Smoothie

This strawberry smoothie is about as simple as it gets. It also tastes really good.

Ingredients

  • Frozen strawberries
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Maybe some ice
  • Maybe a banana

Steps

  1. Get a blender out
  2. Dump strawberries in it and fill it about halfway with water
  3. Maybe put some ice in there so it's cold
  4. Maybe put a banana in there if you want the smoothie to be creamy
  5. Eyeball it and add sugar
  6. Blend it on like pulvurize mode
  7. Add more water if it isn't blending well
  8. Add more sugar to taste
