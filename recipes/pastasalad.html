Pasta Salad

This pasta salad rocks.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice (I recommend rigatoni)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Carrots
  • Onion (yellow? white? I don't know)
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Get a pot of water and boil it
  2. Put pasta in water once it is boiling and boil that to whatever pasta softness you like
  3. While that is happening, cut the carrots, onion, and celery up into little chunks (you have to choose how many and how large you want the chunks to be)
  4. Once the pasta is done, throw it in a bowl with the carrots, onion, and celery
  5. Put a few spoonfuls of mayonnaise on it (to taste)
